Biomedical Projects

Throughout my college experience, I have made it a point to focus on Biomedical projects which can challenge me and make use of the information I have been taught over the past four years in the area of medical instrument design as well as biotechnology development.

Senior Design- Polypectomy Retrieval Device

Worked with an interdisciplinary group of Biomedical Engineering students to develop a polypectomy retrieval device. Specifically, I was involved in the sketching of the engineering drawings of all components, as well as using SolidWorks and other computer softwares to help develop multiple prototypes and iterations of the final product.

Mathematical Model of the Development of Dental Caries

Helped in developing MATLAB code to mathematically model the devleopment of the bacteria S.mutans in rats. This involved designing code that is able to quantifiably model as well as predict the concentration of bacteria growth versus time based on published research.
